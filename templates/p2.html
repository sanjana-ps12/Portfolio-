Milk Management App

This project is a UI/UX prototype created using Figma for a mobile application aimed at improving milk delivery services. The app is designed to help milkmen manage orders efficiently by allowing users to book the quantity of milk they need for doorstep delivery.

Key Features:

  • User Booking Interface: Users can schedule daily or one-time milk deliveries and specify the quantity needed.
  • Delivery Management: Milkmen can track daily orders and plan deliveries to avoid confusion or missed deliveries.
  • Real-Time Order Tracking: Users receive status updates and confirmation once the delivery is made.
  • Customer Profiles: The app maintains profiles with delivery preferences and payment details for easy management.

This UI design aims to reduce delivery errors, save time, and enhance coordination between customers and milk distributors.
